ng-inspector

The AngularJS inspector pane for your browser

Features

  • Scopes

    See whole scope hierarchy in your AngularJS application.

  • DOM

    Mouse-over the scopes in the inspector to highlight associated DOM Nodes.

  • Controllers, directives…

    ng-inspector identifies the Controllers and Directives that triggered the creation of each scope.

  • Models

    See all the scope’s models, their types and values. Click the model to console.log() it’s values.

  • Real time

    As you interact with your application, ng-inspector updates in real time.
